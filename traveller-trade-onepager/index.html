A Few Key Details

BLocal Brokers

Hire one to find goods and negotiate for you. Broker skill 2D/3, grants DM+2 on the deal.

Flat fee: 10% of gross proceeds.

!Illegal Goods & Fixers

Fixers use Streetwise 2D/3, also DM+2 to negotiate, but take 20% of proceeds.

Roll a natural 2 for their skill → they're an informer, agent, or double-crosser.

✕Rejected Deals

Turn down an offered price and you can't return to that supplier or buyer for at least a month.

Each repeat attempt on the same world also carries DM−1.
